About Me

Machine Learning Engineer · Creator · Builder

Hey, I'm Colton. I recently completed my Master's in Computer Science at Georgia Tech with a focus in Machine Learning. I'm all about designing thoughtful systems, solving tough problems, and staying curious while doing it.

I originally came from a finance background, but over the years I fell in love with algorithms, robotics, game development, and deep learning. I’ve built everything from AI for video games to custom trading algorithms, and I’m always experimenting with something new.

I thrive in that intersection between creativity and precision—where you can prototype an idea fast, iterate, and eventually land on something that feels effortless. I value elegance in code, clarity in communication, and a clean design in both UI and logic.

Outside of code, I’m probably messing with electronics, refining my side projects, or casually trying to automate something that probably didn’t need automating (but it’s way cooler now).
